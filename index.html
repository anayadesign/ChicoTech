Get out of the office

There's something for everyone to enjoy in a city that is sunny 249 days out of the year. Recreation, culture, and social life is a part of Chico’s setting.

  • Bidwell Park (4x the size of New York’s Central Park), the Sac River, Feather River Falls, Lake Shasta & Oroville, and Lassen National Forest all in Chico’s backyard.
  • Biking, hiking, swimming, climbing, kayaking, fishing, golfing, disk golf
  • 275 restaurants, 22 Art Venues, over 150 live performances per year, 20 boutiques, 3 seasonal farmers markets.
