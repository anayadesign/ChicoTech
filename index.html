Love where you live

Chico: where the college and the community merge to create a thriving culture. Everywhere you go you’ll see a friendly face and getting around is a breeze! Chico’s longest commute is about 15 minutes, giving you more time to do what you want. The average cost of living is 30% cheaper than the Bay Area, which gives residents more buying power.
